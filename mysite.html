PASTELS for SPRING

Every year, as soon as the first sunny days of spring are announced, like clockwork, people start dusting off the colors they hid in the back of their closets for winter. Shyly but surely streets start filling with pastels of every hue. And while there is nothing groundbreaking about breaking in your 'baby' tones for spring, it still makes you feel like a budding cherryblossom and brings about some much needed seasonal serotonin.
